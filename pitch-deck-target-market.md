## Slide: Target Market & Customer Segments

### Primary Market: Conscious Adventure Travelers ($2.1B Market)

**Segment 1: Eco-Conscious Millennials & Gen Z (Ages 25-45)**
- Income: $75K-150K annually
- Values: Sustainability, authentic experiences, social impact
- Evidence from codebase: Tree planting features, carbon offset calculator, geotagging conservation
- Size: 8.2M potential customers globally
- Behavior: Willing to pay 15-30% premium for sustainable travel

**Segment 2: Affluent Experience Seekers (Ages 35-65)**
- Income: $150K+ annually  
- Values: Luxury with purpose, unique experiences, convenience
- Evidence from codebase: Luxury accommodation options, private vehicle upgrades, helicopter transfers
- Size: 3.1M potential customers globally
- Behavior: High lifetime value ($5K-15K per trip)

**Segment 3: Adventure Groups & Families (Ages 30-55)**
- Income: $100K+ household income
- Values: Shared experiences, safety, educational value
- Evidence from codebase: Group pricing tiers (3-5 people: 12% discount, 6+: 20% discount)
- Size: 2.8M potential customers globally
- Behavior: Book longer trips (5-8 days average)

### Secondary Market: Corporate & Educational Travel

**Segment 4: Corporate Retreat & Incentive Travel**
- Budget: $50K-200K per group
- Evidence from codebase: Team building potential, conservation CSR alignment
- Size: 15K companies globally seeking sustainable travel options

**Segment 5: Educational & Research Groups**
- Budget: $25K-75K per group
- Evidence from codebase: Conservation monitoring, research partnerships, educational value
- Size: 8K institutions globally
